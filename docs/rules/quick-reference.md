## Bonus Formulas

```
Majority Bonus = Stock Price × 10
Minority Bonus = Stock Price × 5
```

## Bonus Tie Rules

| Situation | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| Solo stockholder | Gets BOTH maj + min bonuses |
| Tie for majority | Split (maj + min), round UP to $100, no minority paid |
| Tie for minority | Split minority, round UP to $100 |

## Merger Stock Options

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **HOLD** | Keep stock (worthless until corp founded again) |
| **SELL** | Sell at current price (size before merger) |
| **TRADE** | 2 defunct → 1 survivor (if available) |

## Unplayable Tiles

| Type | Cause | Action |
|------|-------|--------|
| Permanent | Merges 2+ safe corps | Discard, draw new |
| Temporary | Creates 8th corp | Keep in hand |

## Turn Sequence

1. **TRADE** - Trade stock with other players (optional)
2. **PLACE** - Put one tile on matching space
3. **BUY** - Purchase 0-3 stocks (optional)
4. **DRAW** - Take one tile from supply

## Player Trading Rules

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| When | Phase 1 only (before tile placement) |
| Initiator | Active player only |
| Response | Other players may accept or decline |
| Items | Stock, cash, or combinations |

## End Game Triggers

- Any corporation reaches **41+ tiles**, OR
- All corporations on board have **11+ tiles** (all safe)
